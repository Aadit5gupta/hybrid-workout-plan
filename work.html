Aadit’s Elite 8-Day Hybrid Workout Plan

Transform your fitness with this optimized 8-day athletic and aesthetic hybrid split. Get ready to push your limits!

Day 1 – Chest Dominant Push + Speed Core

🕗 AM:
  • Sprint Ladder (10m, 20m, 30m) – 3 rounds
  • Med Ball Slams – 3x12
  • Hanging Leg Raises – 3x12
  • Side Plank Reach – 3x12/side
  • Resistance Band Neck Flexion – 3x15
🕕 PM:
  • Incline DB Press – 1 top set (5–9), 1 back-off (10–15)
  • Machine Chest Press – 3x10–15
  • Cable Fly (Drop Set) – 3x12–15
  • Weighted Dips – 3x8–12
  • Overhead Triceps Extension – 3x8–15
  • Lateral Raises – 4x failure
  • Barbell Shrugs – 3x15 (slow tempo)
  • Neck Curl (lying) – 3x15

Day 2 – Back Thickness + Sprint Conditioning

🕗 AM:
  • 6x20m Sprint Starts (max effort)
  • Dead Bug + Hollow Hold – 2 rounds
  • Ab Wheel Rollout – 3x10
  • Plate Neck Isometric Hold – 3x15s/side
  • Forearm Roller – 3 rounds
🕕 PM:
  • Barbell Row – 1 top, 1 back-off
  • Lat Pulldown – 3x10–15
  • Cable Row – 3x10–15
  • Cable Pullover – 3x12–15
  • Rear Delt Fly – 3x15
  • Rope Curls – 3x12
  • DB Hammer Curl – 2x12
  • Dumbbell Shrugs – 3x15–20
  • Wrist Curls + Reverse Wrist Curls – 3x20 superset

Day 3 – Quad-Focused Legs + Athletic Core

🕗 AM:
  • Agility Ladder + Cone Drill – 5 rounds
  • Sprint Relay (10/20/30m) – 3 rounds
  • Russian Twists (heavy) – 3x20
  • Farmers Carry – 3x30m (heavy)
🕕 PM:
  • Barbell Back Squat – 1 top, 1 back-off
  • Leg Press – 3x10–15
  • Walking Lunges – 2x15/leg
  • Quad Extensions – 3x15
  • Ham Curl – 3x15
  • Standing Calf Raise – 3x20
  • Neck Bridge Hold (optional) – 3x20s

Day 4 – Active Recovery (Mandatory)

AM/PM (light only):

  • 1.5 km jog or incline walk
  • Dynamic mobility: Dead Bug, Bird Dog, Arm Circles
  • Foam Rolling
  • Yoga Flow or Thoracic Spine + Hip Mobility (15–20 mins)

Day 5 – Shoulder Push + Reaction Core

🕗 AM:
  • Reaction Ball Drill – 3x60s
  • BOSU Plank – 3x30s
  • Side Plank with Leg Raise – 3x12/side
  • Neck Harness Extensions – 3x15
🕕 PM:
  • DB Shoulder Press – 1 top, 1 back-off
  • Weighted Dips – 3x8–12
  • Cable Lateral Raise – 3x15
  • Machine Chest Press – 3x10–15
  • Pec Deck – 3x15
  • Overhead Triceps Extension – 3x12
  • Upright Row – 3x12
  • Barbell Shrug Hold (10s top) – 3x12

Day 6 – Back Width + Arms + Core Burnout

🕗 AM:
  • Reactive Sprint Drill – 4x20m
  • Hanging Leg Raise – 3x12
  • Hollow Body Hold – 3x30s
  • Neck Plate Hold (on bench) – 3x30s
  • Towel Hang – 3x30s
🕕 PM:
  • Weighted Pull-Ups – 1 top, 1 back-off
  • T-Bar Row – 3x10–15
  • Lat Pulldown – 3x10–15
  • Rear Delt Fly – 3x15
  • EZ Bar Curl – 3x10
  • Cable Curl (slow negative) – 3x12
  • Wrist Roller or Reverse Curl – 3x20

Day 7 – Hamstring Glute Legs + Explosive Core

🕗 AM:
  • Broad Jumps – 3x6
  • Lateral Bounds – 3x10/side
  • Plank-to-Push-Up – 3x10
  • Farmers Walk Heavy – 3x30s
🕕 PM:
  • Stiff-Leg Deadlift – 1 top, 1 back-off
  • Hack Squat – 3x12
  • Hamstring Curls – 3x15
  • Hip Thrusts – 3x15
  • Quad Extensions – 2x15 (finisher)
  • Seated Calf Raise – 3x20
  • Neck Curl or Shrug Hold – 3x15

Day 8 – Full Rest

  • Sleep 8–9 hrs
  • High protein (2g/kg) + recovery carbs
  • Sauna/massage/mobility (optional)
  • Reflect on strength, sprint, and core progress

⚙️ Weekly Training Focus Strategy

Focus Area Weekly Frequency Notes
Chest 2x Prioritized via incline and machine volume
Shoulders 2x Strength and hypertrophy balanced
Back (Width + Thick) 4x Vertical + horizontal emphasis
Arms 2–3x Alternating emphasis on biceps and triceps
Legs (Quad/Ham Split) 2x Balanced push and pull leg days
Core Daily (AM) Includes both functional and aesthetic work
Neck/Traps/Forearms 3x Strategically spread to avoid CNS fatigue

🧠 Training Smart Tips

  • Prioritize movement quality before intensity to avoid injury.
  • Use AM sessions for speed, agility, and core activation.
  • Leverage PM workouts for strength and hypertrophy with proper rest between sets.
  • Incorporate recovery and mobility to maximize progress and prevent burnout.
  • Track your progress and adjust training loads progressively.
  • Maintain proper nutrition to support training demands and recovery.
